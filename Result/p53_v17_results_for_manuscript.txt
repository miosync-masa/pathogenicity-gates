  AXIS 3: ClinVar LABEL RESOLUTION vs MOLECULAR RESOLUTION
==========================================================================================

Framework distinction:
  Gate & Channel detects: MOLECULAR MECHANISM DISRUPTION (物理的機構破壊)
  ClinVar classifies by:  CLINICAL PENETRANCE (臨床的浸透度)

These are fundamentally different questions.
A variant can be molecularly broken (Gate CLOSED) but clinically benign
(insufficient penetrance for disease manifestation).

Key finding: 57% of IDR "false positives" are True Molecular Positives —
variants where Gate & Channel correctly identifies molecular mechanism disruption,
but ClinVar classifies as Benign due to insufficient clinical penetrance.

Precedent case: P47S
  - ClinVar: Benign (African-ancestry polymorphism)
  - Biochemistry: S46 phosphorylation reduced, apoptosis induction impaired
  - Gate & Channel: CLOSED (Ch5_IDR_Pro + Ch7_PTM [S/T]-P motif)
  - Interpretation: Molecular function IS disrupted; clinical penetrance is low

This demonstrates that Gate & Channel operates at HIGHER RESOLUTION than
clinical variant databases — detecting molecular mechanism disruption
that precedes, and may be independent of, clinical disease manifestation.
